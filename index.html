    QuickNotify

    QuickNotify is a quick way to create Mac OS X notifications. It was created to be easy and streamlined.

    Usage

    To create a new notification, simply click on the menu bar icon with the QuickNotify icon (like this: ). You will be greeted with a dialog that has the categories Title, Subtitle, Message, Seconds, Minutes, and Hours. Title is the large title at the top of the notification, Subtitle is directly beneath it, and, finally, Message is the smallest and lowest text area. Seconds, Minutes, and Hours denote the delay until the notification appears. To send it to the notification centre, just click send, or click cancel if you decide that you don't want to send it after all.
